The Value

Why Partner with TEDxXinyi 為何與 TEDxXinyi 合作

💡

Brand with ideas 與好點子站在一起

Align your brand with Ideas Worth Spreading and a global TEDx community that values curiosity, creativity, and impact.

把品牌與「好點子、創造力與社會影響力」連結在一起，走進以故事與內容為核心的國際社群。

🔗

Engage an idea-driven community 接觸行動派社群

Reach participants who care about the future, innovation, and meaningful conversations — ideal for brand experiences and product trials.

參與者關心未來、創新與議題，是最適合品牌體驗與產品試用的一群人。

🌱

Create lasting impact 讓影響力延續活動之外

Talks and stories from TEDxXinyi continue to live online, in media, and in communities long after the event night.

講者故事與內容在活動後，會持續透過影片、社群與媒體被看見與分享。
