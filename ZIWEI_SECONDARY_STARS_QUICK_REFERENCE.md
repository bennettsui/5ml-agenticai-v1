## At-a-Glance Star Summary

### Void Stars (虛星)

#### 旬空 (Xunkong) - Cyclic Void ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Element:** Water (水) | **Yin-Yang:** Yin | **Nature:** 凶 (Malevolent)
- **Core:** Temporary emptiness, things fail to materialize in that period
- **Best Case:** Reduces harmful effects of malefic stars
- **Worst Case:** Good fortune falls through (吉化為凶)
- **Frequency:** HIGH in texts | **Research:** COMPLETE

#### 截空 (Jiekong) - Interception Void ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Element:** Fire (火) | **Yin-Yang:** Yang | **Nature:** 凶 (Malevolent - with reverse effect)
- **Core:** Permanent cut-off, black hole effect that absorbs energy
- **Best Case:** Neutralizes malefic stars, reducing disasters
- **Worst Case:** Auspicious outcomes blocked/cut off (吉化為凶)
- **Unique:** Reverse mechanism - "見吉化凶，見凶化吉"
- **Frequency:** HIGH in texts | **Research:** COMPLETE

### Romance & Marriage Stars

#### 紅鸞 (Hong Luan) - Red Phoenix ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Element:** Water (癸水) | **Yin-Yang:** Yin | **Nature:** 吉 (Auspicious)
- **Core:** Marriage indicator, romantic attraction, peach blossom luck
- **Best Case:** Early marriage with good spouse, strong personal charm
- **Worst Case:** Overly romantic, obsessive emotions
- **Key:** MUST pair with Tian Xi for complete analysis
- **Frequency:** VERY HIGH | **Research:** COMPLETE

#### 天喜 (Tian Xi) - Heavenly Joy ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Element:** Water (壬水) | **Yin-Yang:** Yang | **Nature:** 吉 (Auspicious)
- **Core:** Joy, celebration, fertility, happiness
- **Best Case:** Multiple children, celebratory wealth (喜慶之財)
- **Worst Case:** Overconfidence, arrogance, laziness
- **Key:** ALWAYS analyze with Hong Luan - they're a pair
- **Frequency:** VERY HIGH | **Research:** COMPLETE

**⭐ COMBINATION: 紅鸞天喜 = STRONGEST Romance Indicator**
```
When together: Perfect romantic fortune, happy marriage, multiple children
Palace effect: Marriage palace shows highest romance success
Wealth palace: Creates "celebratory wealth" (marriages/births/celebrations)
```

- **Key:** Pairs with Left Assistant (左輔) - best in same palace

- **Best Case:** High social status, official recognition, rapid advancement
- **Worst Case:** Vanity-driven, overly reputation-conscious
- **Key:** Pairs with Right Assistant (右弼) - best in same palace
- **Modern:** Corporate recognition, job promotion, awards, social status
- **Frequency:** MEDIUM-HIGH | **Research:** COMPLETE

## Key Mechanics to Remember

### 1. Main Star is Always Primary
Secondary stars AMPLIFY or REFINE main star effects - they don't override them.

### 2. Combinations > Individual Stars
Single secondary star is good; paired secondary stars are MUCH stronger.

### 3. Void Stars Need Reverse Thinking
Don't assume 旬空 and 截空 are bad - check what they're affecting first.

### 4. Context Matters
- Same star has different meaning in different palaces
- Palace position is as important as the star itself

### 5. Luck Cycles Matter
Some secondary stars (especially void stars) have MAJOR effect on 大運 and 流年.
